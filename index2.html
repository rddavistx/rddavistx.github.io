About

Results-driven web developer with a passion for creativity and problem solving.

After 26 years in the classroom as a band director, I decided to take a leap of faith and go for a career as a full stack web developer. I have always had a passion for creativity and problem solving, which makes web development a perfect fit for me.

My previous experience includes working as a web developer at 360Connect, where I was responsible for building and maintaining the company's websites, as well as testing for optimization and cross browser compatibilty.

Before that, I completed a 12-week immersive training program at General Assembly designed to teach the fundamentals of full-stack programming. In this class, I learned how to build quality programs with teams and on individual projects. I gained valuable experience working with HTML/CSS, Javascript and JQuery, databases, Express, ReactJS, PHP, Wordpress, and other frameworks.
